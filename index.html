1957

The first year of operations of Fairfield Little League and James Bay-City Centre Little League in the City of Victoria. Neither league charged registration fees in their first year. In 1958, James Bay City Centre began charging a $1 fee to cover insurance. Both leagues continued operating until they combined in 1973.

1973

Fairfield (orange and white) and James Bay-City Centre (black and white) combined to form Beacon Hill Little League (orange and black) within Little League District 2.

We even had an All-Star team!
